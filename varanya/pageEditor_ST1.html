SHOP

The Shop Page plays a crucial role on the website, offering visitors a platform to discover and buy a curated selection of health and wellness products.

LAYOUT & DESIGN: The layout of the page is crafted to be visually attractive and easy to navigate, with a user-friendly design that is clear and intuitive. Products are showcased in a grid layout, facilitating seamless browsing of the available items.

PRODUCT PRESENTATION: Every product is showcased alongside an image, its name, and price, enabling users to swiftly identify items of interest and review essential information before proceeding with a purchase.

INTERACTIVE FEATURES: Within the Shop, interactive features like quantity selectors and an "Add to Cart" button are incorporated for each product. This allows users to easily adjust the quantity of items they want to buy and add them to their cart with a simple click.

DYNAMIC PRICING: As users change the quantity of items in their cart, the total price is automatically updated, giving immediate feedback on the overall cost of the selected items in real-time.

CART MANAGEMENT: Additionally, the page provides functionality to manage the shopping cart, including a 'reset cart' button to clear the contents and begin anew.

CSS STYLING: CSS is utilized to improve the visual aesthetics of the page by specifying fonts, colors, layout, and other stylistic elements. This guarantees a uniform and appealing user experience across various devices and screen sizes.

JAVASCRIPT FUNCTIONALITY: JavaScript is employed to introduce interactivity and dynamic functionality to the Shop Page, incorporating features like adjusting quantities, managing the cart, and dynamically calculating prices. These enhancements elevate the user's shopping experience.
